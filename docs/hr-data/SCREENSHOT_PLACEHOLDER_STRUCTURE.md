## 3. Image Standards
- **Format**: PNG or WebP optimized for web.
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080 (1080p).
- **Sanitization**: Ensure all Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is blurred or mocked before committing screenshots to the repository.
